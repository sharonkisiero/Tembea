Destination

Nairobi

Experience vibrant city life, cultural hotspots, and nearby nature parks in Kenya’s capital

Diani

Diani Beach is a popular tourist destination located on the south coast of Kenya, approximately 30 kilometers south of Mombasa. It is known for its stunning white-sand beaches, turquoise waters, and vibrant coral reefs, making it a popular destination for beach lovers and water sports enthusiast

Kitale

A peaceful town in western Kenya, Kitale offers a taste of countryside life with beautiful farms, forests, and cool weather. Perfect for travelers looking for a calm and quiet retreat away from busy city life.

Mt Elgon

Mt. Elgon is a hidden gem for nature lovers and hikers. Enjoy mountain treks, caves, and amazing views of waterfalls and wildlife. Ideal for eco-tourists and adventurers who love scenic trails and cooler climates.

Maasai Mara

One of the world s most famous safari destinations,go off roads. With Tembea Kenya, you can now book guided motorbike safaris — a thrilling new way to explore the savannah, spot lions, elephants, and the Big Five up close. Best for adrenaline lovers!
